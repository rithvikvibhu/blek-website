Response Time SLA

Effective January 1, 2025

Why This SLA Exists

Over the past year, I've noticed that I haven't been as responsive as I'd like to be. Life gets busy, messages pile up, and before you know it, weeks have passed without a reply.

As a 2025 New Year's resolution, I want to change this pattern. I'm making a formal commitment to be more responsive, and I'm putting my money/food where my mouth is!

Coverage

  • Platforms: WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack, Discord, and Email (but not Microsoft Teams and Reddit)
  • Applies to first response only in any new conversation
  • Valid indefinitely until cancelled

Response Time Commitment

  • During IST Daytime (8:00 AM - 11:00 PM IST): 4 hours
  • During IST Nighttime (11:00 PM - 8:00 AM IST): 16 hours
  • All Other Time Zones: 16 hours
