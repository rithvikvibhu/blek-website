Minimal page that finds all registrars where a TLD is listed.

Meant for [Handshake](https://handshake.org) names, especially those staked with [Namebase Registry](https://namebase.io).

Supported Registrars:

- Namecheap
- Encirca
- Porkbun
- 101Domain
