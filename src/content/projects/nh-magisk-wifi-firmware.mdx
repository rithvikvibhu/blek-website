[Kali NetHunter](https://www.kali.org/get-kali/#kali-mobile) supports plenty of external adapters - if your kernel ships the drivers and you have the right firmware.

This [Magisk](https://github.com/topjohnwu/Magisk) module bundles the missing blobs for dozens of chipsets (Ralink RT2xxx/RT3xxx, Realtek RTL8188/8192/8822, Atheros AR9170/7010, MediaTek MT7601u, Broadcom 43xx, and more) and drops them into place during boot so you can plug-and-play adapters like the TL-WN722N or AWUS036NEH.
