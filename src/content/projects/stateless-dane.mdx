[Stateless DANE](https://hsd-dev.org/HIPs/proposals/0017/) is a way to secure your website with self-signed certificates, without being blessed by anyone else. The chain of trust is rooted in DNS (with DNSSEC securing it).

`stateless-dane` is a CLI, library, and utility for dealing with Stateless DANE certificates.

As a CLI, it can be used to generate and inspect Stateless DANE certificates.

As a Library, it can be integrated into resolvers, servers, apps, and anywhere else. (for example: [HTools Certificate Authority](https://acme.htools.work))
