# Response Time SLA

**Effective January 1, 2025**

> ### Why This SLA Exists
>
> Over the past year, I've noticed that I haven't been as responsive as
> I'd like to be. Life gets busy, messages pile up, and before you know
> it, weeks have passed without a reply.
>
> As a 2025 New Year's resolution, I want to change this pattern. I'm
> making a formal commitment to be more responsive, and I'm putting my
> money/food where my mouth is!

## Coverage

- Platforms: WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack, Discord, and Email (but not
  Microsoft Teams and Reddit)
- Applies to first response only in any new conversation
- Valid indefinitely until cancelled

## Response Time Commitment

- During IST Daytime (8:00 AM - 11:00 PM IST): 4 hours
- During IST Nighttime (11:00 PM - 8:00 AM IST): 16 hours
- All Other Time Zones: 16 hours

## Compensation

If I fail to respond within the specified timeframe:

- You get one free meal with me
- Can be breakfast, lunch, dinner, brunch, midnight snack, or any other
  food gathering

## Fine Print

- This SLA covers initial response time only
- No guarantee of task completion or delivery
- Limited to one meal compensation per violation
- Response time clock starts when message is received
- Automatic holiday exemptions apply for major festivals
- I reserve the right to cancel or modify this SLA with public notice
